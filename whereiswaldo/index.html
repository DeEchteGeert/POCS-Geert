What is Effenarium?

Effenarium is a digital token that you can earn by interacting with the festival.

How can you earn Effenarium?

You can earn Effenarium by watching the Hit the city live-stream. You will earn 50 Effenarium every 60 minutes of watching the live-stream, but the more bonus Effenarium you will earn. For instance, if you comment a lot you can earn a random 10 Effenarium.

What can you do with Effenarium?

With Effenarium you can buy many great rewards. You can buy merch, unlock demo or unreleased versions of a song or vote wich song the headliners should play to name a few.
